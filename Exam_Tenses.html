PART 2

Speaking — Fluency & Tenses

🎤 ~10 minutes
Answer each question out loud in full sentences. You are graded on fluency and the correct, varied use of tenses — so try to use a range of past, present and future forms.
1
Tell me about a memorable trip or event from your past. What happened, and what were you doing at the time? past tenses
2
Describe your typical week. What do you usually do, and what are you working on these days? present simple / continuous
3
What is something you have achieved or learned recently? How long have you been doing it? present perfect
4
What are your plans for the next year, and what do you think will happen in the world? going to / will
5
Imagine your life in five years. What will you be doing, and what will you have achieved by then? future continuous / perfect
💡 Take a few seconds to think before each answer. Speaking naturally and keeping going matters as much as being perfectly accurate.
