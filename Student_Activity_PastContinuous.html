Part 1 — Find the Past Continuous 🔍

Read the story carefully. Click the words that form a Past Continuous verb. Remember — Past Continuous needs TWO words: was/were + a verb ending in -ing. No underlines this time — find them yourself! 👀 Then press Check.

Part 2 — Choose the correct form ✍️

Pick Past Continuous (was/were + -ing) for the long action, or Past Simple for the short/finished one.

Part 3 — when or while? 🔗

Remember: while + the long action (was/were + -ing) · when + the short action (Past Simple).
