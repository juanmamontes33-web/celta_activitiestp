Present Simple — your usual life

Habits, routines, facts, permanent things.

Simple ⏰
WE USE IT FOR
🔁 habits/routines: "I get up at 7."
🌍 facts: "Water boils at 100°."
🏠 permanent: "She lives in Madrid."
⏰ always · usually · often · sometimes · never · every day
FORM
➕ I/you/we/they work · he/she/it works
➖ don't / doesn't + verb
❓ Do / Does + subject + verb?
⚠️ Don't forget the -s for he/she/it! "He work" → "He works" ✅  (Spanish has no -s here — easy to forget!)
🔵

Present Continuous — happening now

Now, around now, temporary, changing.

Continuous 🔵
WE USE IT FOR
⏱️ now: "I'm studying right now."
📅 temporary: "I'm staying with friends this week."
📈 changing: "The weather is getting colder."
🔵 now · right now · at the moment · today · these days
FORM
am / is / are + verb-ing
I'm working · she's working · they're working
🇪🇸 like "estoy trabajando" — estar + gerundio!
⚖️

So… which one?

Permanent & usual, or temporary & now?

Choose 🧭
🟡 In general / permanent
"I live in Mexico."
"She works at a bank."
🔵 Temporary / right now
"I'm living with my parents (for now)."
"She's working from home today."
🟡 always true / a habit → Simple  ·  🔵 just now / just these days → Continuous
⛔

Careful! Some verbs hate -ing

Stative verbs stay in the simple — even for "now".

Watch out ⛔
Verbs about thoughts & feelings (not actions) don't use the continuous:
want know like / love need understand believe
I'm wanting a coffee. ❌
I want a coffee. ✅

Speaking — Mime & Guess!

A fun camera game for now-actions.

Game 🎭
🔵 Round 1 — Mime it! (now)
One person mimes an action on camera. The other guesses:
"Are you cooking?" → "Yes, I'm cooking!" 🍳
swimming · driving · sleeping · dancing · texting…
🟡 Round 2 — In common (usually)
Find 3 things you BOTH do every day:
"I drink coffee. Do you?" → "Yes! I drink coffee too!" ☕
Use he/she + -s when you report back!
