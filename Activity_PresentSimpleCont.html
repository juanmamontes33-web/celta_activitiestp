Present Simple & Present Continuous

Usual life or happening now? Three parts — check your answers as you go.

1
Simple or Continuous? ✍️
2
Which is correct? ✅
3
Now or usually? 🧠

Part 1 — Simple or Continuous? ✍️

Look at the time word! every day / usually → Simple · now / today → Continuous.

Part 2 — Which sentence is correct? ✅

Watch for: the -s (he/she/it), am/is/are with -ing, do/does, and stative verbs (want, know).

Part 3 — Now or usually? 🧠

Read each sentence. Is it about right now / temporary, or something in general / a habit?
