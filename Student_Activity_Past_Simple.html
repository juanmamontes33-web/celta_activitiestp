3How does Past Simple work?

Three forms: Affirmative, Negative, Question. After didn't and Did, always use the base verb!
✓ Affirmative
Subject + verb (past form)
I walked home.
She visited Paris.
We went out.
✗ Negative
Subject + didn't + base verb
I didn't walk.
She didn't visit.
We didn't go.
? Question
Did + subject + base verb?
Did you walk?
Did she visit?
Did we go?
