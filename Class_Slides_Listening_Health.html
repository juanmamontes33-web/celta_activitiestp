Key words · before you listen
ADJECTIVE · /ˈsedəntri/
sedentary
involving a lot of sitting and little physical activity.
CCQ: An office job — sit a lot or move a lot? Sit
NOUN
a trade-off
you accept a disadvantage in order to get a benefit.
CCQ: Do you gain something AND give up something? ✓
IDIOM
more bang for your buck
better results for the same effort you put in.
CCQ: More value or less? More · Same effort? ✓
