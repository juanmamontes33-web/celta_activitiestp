VOCABULARY
Key words · before you listen
NOUN · /ˌædəˈlesnt/
adolescent
a young person aged 10–19, between childhood and adulthood.
CCQ: A 15-year-old? ✓ · A 40-year-old? ✗
NOUN · /ˈælɡərɪðəm/
algorithm
a set of rules an app uses to choose and personalise what you see.
CCQ: Does it pick content for you? ✓ · Same for everyone? ✗
NOUN · /ˈrevənjuː/
revenue
the money a company earns — for example, from advertising.
CCQ: Money in or out? · Where from? Adverts!
