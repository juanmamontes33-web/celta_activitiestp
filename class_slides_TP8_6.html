First read — the model

Read fast. What is it FOR?

PUERTO LUCÍA — Your Perfect Escape
Nestled in the heart of the Pacific coast, Puerto Lucía offers golden beaches, colourful streets and some of the freshest seafood you’ll ever taste. Whether you’re looking for adventure or just a quiet weekend away, there’s something for everyone.

Spend your mornings exploring the old harbour and its lively fish market. In the afternoon, hike up to the lighthouse for breathtaking views. Don’t miss the Saturday night food festival, where local families share traditional dishes under the stars.

Puerto Lucía is just two hours by bus from the capital. The best time to visit is March–June. Rooms start at $40 a night, breakfast included.

Why not treat yourself to the getaway you deserve? Book today at puertolucia.example

4 phrases that sell

Discover · match each phrase to its job

In pairs 🤝
1 · “Nestled in the heart of the coast…”
2 · “Whether you’re A or B, there’s something for everyone.”
3 · “Don’t miss the food festival.”
4 · “Why not treat yourself…?”
Its job is to…
…strongly recommend a highlight
…say where the place is (attractively)
…invite the reader to act (call to action)
…show it suits every kind of visitor
✅

What kind of word is each part?

Answer key · form + register

Answer key 🔑
the coast = PLACE · Puerto Lucía = SUBJECT · offers… = VERB + COMPLEMENT  ·  written / promotional
looking for adventure & just relaxing = both GERUND (-ING) PHRASES  ·  inclusive
the Saturday night food festival = NOUN PHRASE  ·  strong rec. — NOT “don’t miss to…”
treat yourself = BASE VERB · to a weekend away = OBJECT  ·  call to action — NOT “why not to…”
Read the sentence and decide what KIND of word or phrase each part is — never “to + verb” after Don’t miss / Why not. ✨
