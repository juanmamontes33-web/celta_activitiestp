VOCABULARY
Key words · learn before you read
NOUN · /tjuˈɪʃn fiːz/
tuition fees
the money you pay to study at university.
CCQ: Pay it to the shop, or the university? · For studying?
NOUN · /əˈpren.tɪs.ʃɪp/
apprenticeship
a job where you learn a skill while working — and get paid.
CCQ: Do you get paid? ✓ · Learn while working? ✓ · Only in class? ✗
NOUN PHRASE
soft skills
personal abilities like communication and teamwork (not technical).
CCQ: Is "teamwork" a soft skill? ✓ · Is "coding" a soft skill? ✗

TASK 1 · GIST
Read fast · the big idea
First read — for the main idea 🎯
📖 Open the article. Read it quickly — about 3 minutes. Don't stop at every new word.
