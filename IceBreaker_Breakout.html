Get to Know Each Other 💬

Conversation cards · perfect for pairs or groups of three

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 In your breakout room

  1. One person shares their screen — or just look at this together.
  2. Type everyone's names below (optional) so the card knows whose turn it is.
  3. Press Draw a question. The person it names answers — say as much as you can!
  4. The others react, agree or disagree, and ask a follow-up question.
  5. Press Draw again to pass it to the next person. Keep going until your teacher calls you back! 🎉
Separate names with commas. Leave empty to just take turns.
