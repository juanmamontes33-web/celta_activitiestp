Superlatives · Part 202 / 11
PATTERN · 1
one of the + superlative + plural
Not #1 — but in the top group 🏅
"Walking is one of the best exercises for beginners."
"Sugar is one of the worst things for your teeth."
Pattern: one of the + superlative + PLURAL noun
💡 Tip: the noun must be plural — "one of the best exercises", not "one of the best exercise". (There are several great ones — it's one of them!)

Superlatives · Part 204 / 11
PATTERN · 3
by far the … · making it stronger
When #1 wins by a big margin 💪
"Water is by far the healthiest drink."
"Sleep is by far the most important habit."
💡 by far = the difference is huge — not close! You can also say "easily the best". Both go before "the".
Superlatives · Part 205 / 11
PATTERN · 4
the second / third + superlative
Not #1, but #2 or #3 🥈🥉
"Swimming is the second healthiest exercise."
"That's the third most popular diet in the world."
Pattern: the + second / third + superlative
💡 Tip: just add the ordinal after "the": the second biggest, the third most expensive. Keep the rest the same!
Superlatives · Part 206 / 11
TIPS
Four tips to sound natural
1
After "one of the …", the noun is plural: "one of the best exercises".
2
"the … I've ever …" = present perfect + a past participle (eaten, tried, seen).
3
by far / easily go before "the" to stress a big lead.
4
🔊 Keep the /ðə/ and the -est ending weak — stress the adjective.
Superlatives · Part 207 / 11
READ ALOUD
Read in pairs · the new patterns
💬 The healthiest meal
A: This is the best salad I've ever eaten!
B: Right? Avocado is one of the healthiest foods you can eat.
A: And it's by far the tastiest thing here.
B: Agreed. Fish is good too — probably the second healthiest option.
💬 Ways to relax
A: Yoga is the most relaxing thing I've ever tried.
B: For me, sleep is by far the most important thing.
A: True! And walking is one of the easiest ways to reduce stress.
B: Definitely the cheapest, too!
🎯 Swap roles and read again. Watch the plural after "one of the…" and the V3 after "I've ever…".
