Your turn to talk
Tell & compare 💬
Round 1 · Tell your story
Tell your partner about a time something dramatic or unexpected happened to you.
Use: "was just …-ing when" · "all of a sudden" · "in the end"
Round 2 · New partner · Compare
Tell your new partner about the stories you just heard.
Use: "According to…" · "claimed that…" · "was absolutely certain that…"
Watch me first 👀
"Last year I was just walking home when, all of a sudden, a dog ran past with a sausage in its mouth! In the end the owner caught it.

According to my neighbour, the dog was hers — but the butcher claimed that it was a street dog!"
✍️ First — plan! (1–2 min)
Write 4–5 key words for your story — not full sentences. What happened? The surprise? The ending?
