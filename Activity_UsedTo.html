Part 1 — used to or didn't use to? ✍️

Read the clue and choose the correct form. used to = it was true before · didn't use to = it was NOT true before.

Part 2 — Which sentence is correct? ✅

Watch the disappearing "d"! After didn't and Did, we write use to. And always use the base verb.
