VOCABULARY
Key words · learn before you read
ADJECTIVE · /əˈdɪktɪv/
addictive
something you want to do again and again and find hard to stop.
"Games can be really addictive."
CCQ: Easy or hard to stop? · Do you want more?
NOUN PHRASE
the attention economy
the idea that companies compete to win and keep your attention — because your time is worth money.
CCQ: What do they want? · Is your time worth money?
PHRASAL VERB
push back
to resist or oppose something you don't like.
"Users pushed back against the new ads."
CCQ: Accept it, or resist it?
