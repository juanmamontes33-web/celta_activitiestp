Continuous (activity) vs Simple (result)

Activity & how long, or result & how many? Three parts — check your answers as you go.

1
Activity or Result? ✍️
2
Which is correct? ✅
3
What does it mean? 🧠

Part 1 — Activity or Result? Choose the form ✍️

Continuous = the activity / how long · Simple = the finished result / how many. Read the clue and choose!

Part 2 — Which sentence is correct? ✅

Watch for: how long → continuous · how many → simple · and stative verbs (know, have, be) → simple only!

Part 3 — What does it really mean? 🧠

Read each sentence. Is the focus the ACTIVITY (maybe not finished) or the RESULT (finished)?
