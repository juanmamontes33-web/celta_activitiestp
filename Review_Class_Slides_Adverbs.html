WHERE do they go? — the golden rule

This is the part most students get wrong. Two simple rules!

Position 📍
RULE 1 · with normal verbs
adverb goes BEFORE the verb
I usually walk to work. 🚶
RULE 2 · with the verb 'be'
adverb goes AFTER be
She is often tired. 😴
⚠️ THE COMMON MISTAKE (careful — this is the Spanish habit!)
I drink always coffee. ❌
Always I drink coffee. ❌
→ I always drink coffee. ✅
⏪

In YOUR stories — adverbs + the past

Same position rule — just put the verb in the past!

Past 🔁
We always went to the beach in summer. 🏖️
She never wanted to leave early. 😅
My family often visited my grandparents. 👵
