VOCABULARY
Key words · before you listen
IDIOM
take (it) with a pinch of salt
to not fully believe something, because it may not be true.
CCQ: Do you believe it 100%? ✗ · A bit doubtful? ✓
NOUN PHRASE
a track record
someone's past successes or failures — what they've done before.
CCQ: Past or future? Past · Good record = done well before? ✓
NOUN · /dɪsˈtəʊpiən/
a dystopian
someone who imagines a dark, frightening future of suffering.
CCQ: Happy future or nightmare? Nightmare ✗😱
